## 🖌 Overview  
Calibre Advanced is a comprehensive ebook library management application that provides format conversion, metadata editing, content server, and device syncing for readers who maintain large ebook collections across multiple devices and formats.

Format conversion handles conversion between EPUB, MOBI, AZW, PDF, and many other ebook formats with configurable output quality, making it possible to read any ebook on any device regardless of the original format or device ecosystem.

Metadata editing applies accurate title, author, series, cover, and description information to any ebook using a built-in editor and online metadata fetching that retrieves correct information from databases automatically.

Content server serves your library over the local network to browsers and compatible reader applications, making your entire ebook collection accessible on any device in your home without manual file transfer.

For readers who maintain large digital libraries, use multiple reading devices, and want complete control over their ebook collection and format compatibility, Calibre Advanced is the ebook management standard that the reading community has relied on for comprehensive library control.

## 🚀 Why Choose Calibre Advanced?

Calibre Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Calibre Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
